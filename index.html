About Me

QUALITY
MINDSET

I'm a software tester focused on delivering reliable, user-ready software. I don't just check if features work — I challenge them to understand how they fail under real-world conditions.

My approach centers on hunting edge cases and identifying high-risk defects that could impact users, business flow, or trust in the product — prioritizing by risk and impact, not checklists.

Great testing is about communication as much as execution. I translate technical issues into clear, actionable insights so stakeholders can make confident decisions.
